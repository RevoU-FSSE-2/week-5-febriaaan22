WILLY CHAVARRIA

FW 23 KANGAROO

"a story of protection in a cruel and harsh world. A modern classics that challenged Eurocentric standards of beauty, elegance and style consisting of mostly all-black garments. With hints of gardenia white in blouses, flowers, pussy bows, scarves, and suit panels, the collection was a body of art that billowed, crinkled, and flowed across the audience-made runway." - Willy on inspiration for FW23 Kangaroo

SS23 PLEASE RISE

"he collection was actually going to be much darker at the onset, but then it started to become a story of good and evil. Ideally, my collections are about making us realize that we are the best, that we are beautiful, that we are good." - Willy on inspiration for SS23 Please Rise.

FW22 UNCUT

"When I was building this collection, I wanted to create a connection between what we see worn on the streets and our higher consciousness. I thought it was important to show ourselves in a spiritual context to highlight a kind of supreme beauty." - Willy on inspiration for FW22 Uncut.
